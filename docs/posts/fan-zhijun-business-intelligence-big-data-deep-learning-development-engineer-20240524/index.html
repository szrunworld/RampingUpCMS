2018/06-2018/10 Precipitation and Temperature Prediction Model for Extended Period, Guizhou Meteorological Bureau

Project description:

The extension period refers to the time period of 10 to 30 days in the future, which is at the junction of weather and climate. The prediction limit of the weather dynamic system is within 10 days, and the accuracy of the prediction after 10 days will sharply decrease; while the climate prediction scale is generally after 30 days. Therefore, predicting 10 to 30 days has become a difficult problem in meteorology. The purpose of this project is to use Deep learning technology to develop models for predicting precipitation and temperature during the extension period based on historical meteorological data. Due to the low accuracy of atmospheric dynamic system prediction during the extension period, the current extension period prediction mainly relies on human experience combined with various model prediction results to give approximate directional predictions, with very low accuracy. Deep learning technologies such as convolution networks (CNN and ResNet) and recurrent networks (RNN and LSTM) are used to mine data in multiple dimensions, learn deeper data features, and predict more accurate rainfall or cooling processes.
